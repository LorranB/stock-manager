\# 📱 Estoque Manager



A smart and automated \*\*pricing management application\*\* designed for smartphone and tablet retailers, built to transform raw inventory data into actionable pricing insights.



The project automatically reads supplier inventory PDFs, searches competitors' prices on \*\*Boadica\*\*, calculates optimized selling prices, and generates professional reports ready for operational use.



Developed by \*\*Bruno Lorran Sanches do Vale\*\* in collaboration with \*\*ChatGPT\*\*, this project was created as a practical experiment to combine \*\*automation, business intelligence, and user-centered software design\*\* into a single workflow.



\---



\## 🧭 Overview



Pricing hundreds of products manually is time-consuming, repetitive, and highly prone to human error.



The \*\*Estoque Manager\*\* was developed to simplify this process by automating inventory analysis from start to finish:



\* Reads supplier inventory directly from PDF files;

\* Identifies products and their specifications automatically;

\* Searches competitor prices from the Boadica marketplace;

\* Calculates strategic selling prices based on custom pricing rules;

\* Generates WhatsApp-ready product lists for customers;

\* Exports structured reports and historical pricing data.



> 💡 What used to take hours of manual work can now be completed in just a few minutes.
